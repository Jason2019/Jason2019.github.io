Jason Tao

Jason Tao, a Shanghai local resident, went to high school in Shanghai High School. After that, he was admitted to NYU Shanghai, where students "make the world your major". Now, he is a rising senior, double major in Economics and Business and Finance. He was awarded Dean's List Honor over the 2017-2017 Academic Year. He is very active in team sports. In 2016 fall, he led NYU Shanghai flag football team to win the second place in NFL China Shanghai regional league. During his study away in NYU Abu Dhabi, he joined NYUAD rugby team and helped the team secure the second place in the Dubai Mixed Touch League.
